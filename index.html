FAQs

Know more, Worry less.

1. Who can buy Incep mobile insurance?

Anyone with a valid mobile invoice can purchase our insurance for just ₹999.(₹999 offer is valid only for limited period.)

2. What does Incep insurance cover?

We cover 50% of your mobile’s value in case of lost or theft.

3. How do I file a claim?

Simply follow these steps:
1. File an FIR at your nearest police station.
2. Fill out our online claim form and upload the required documents.
3. Once verified, receive 50% of your mobile’s value in your bank account.

4. What documents are required to claim insurance?

You’ll need:
1. A copy of the FIR
2. The original invoice of your mobile
3. A valid ID proof

5. How long does it take to process a claim?

Most claims are processed within 48 hours after document verification. Sometimes it takes upto 7 business days for payout settlement.

6. Can I insure an old mobile?

Yes, but you can only insure mobile phones purchased after 31 December 2020. An original invoice are required for insurance.

7. What if I lose my invoice?

The original invoice is required to file a claim. If you’ve lost it, check with the retailer or online store where you purchased the mobile.

8. Is there a time limit to file a claim after lost or theft?

Yes, you must file a claim within 48 hours of losing your phone. Click on "Start a Claim" button available at Claim Center Section for Claim Form.

9. How do I contact support?

You can reach us via email for any assistance regarding claims or policies.

10. How do I track my claim status?

You can track your claim status through your account on our website or contact our support team.
